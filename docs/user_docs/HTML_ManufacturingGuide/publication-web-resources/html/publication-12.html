Assembly

x-axis

Slot the x body through the y part.

Note: The two flat sides should be facing towards each other as shown.

Slot the x stage part into the square receptacle on the x stage body.

Note: The larger radius on the x body fits into the slot on the top of the x part

Secure the two S Stage Parts together with one of the low profile M3 screws, Do NOT overtighten!

Grab 2x X Stage Clamps and screw them into the X Part where the two holes are, they will hold your sample plate in place, Do NOT overtighten!
